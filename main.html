Summary

"The Dark Knight" is a masterpiece directed by Christopher Nolan that follows Batman's battle against the Joker, a criminal mastermind whose chaotic schemes test Gotham City's resilience. The movie explores themes of morality, sacrifice, and the blurred lines between heroism and villainy.

Actors

The movie stars Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne/Batman, Heath Ledger as the Joker, and Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent. Heath Ledger's portrayal of the Joker is widely regarded as one of the greatest performances in cinema history.
